Reverberation Time Calculator (T60)

Find the T60 of your room and get recommendations for acoustic treatment!

Reverberation Time is the time it takes for a sound to decay by 60 dB and is sometimes abbreviated T60 or RT60. A T60 of less than 0.5 seconds is ideal for recording studios, home theaters, and anywhere good speech clarity is desired.
